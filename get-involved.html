Frequently Asked Questions

How do I volunteer? +
Fill out the volunteer form above and our team will contact you with opportunities that match your skills and availability.
Can I donate in-kind items? +
Yes! We accept non-monetary donations such as clothing, books, and food. Please email us for details.
Is my donation tax-deductible? +
SM's Foundation is registered as a non-profit organization, and donations are tax-deductible. Contact us for your donation receipt.
