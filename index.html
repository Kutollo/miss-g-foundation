About Us

Miss G Foundation is a youth-centered, faith-driven nonprofit based in Bushbuckridge. We focus on education, mentorship, and community transformation through purpose and spiritual growth.

Our Programs

  • Motivation & Career Talks: School partnerships for learner development.
  • Church Programs: Worship nights and Sunday school support.
  • Community Campaigns: Awareness on gender, mental health, and more.
  • University Help: Application and mentorship for post-school opportunities.

Mission, Vision & Values

Mission: To empower youth and families through holistic development and spiritual purpose.

Vision: A generation rising in purpose, leading transformation across South Africa.

Values: Faith, Compassion, Empowerment, Integrity, and Community.
