About Us

Miss G Foundation is a youth-centered, faith-driven nonprofit in Bushbuckridge, South Africa. Founded by Miss G, our mission is to inspire and equip youth and families with the tools, hope, and guidance they need to succeed through education, mentorship, and faith-based community development.
