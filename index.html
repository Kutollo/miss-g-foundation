About Us

Miss G Foundation is a youth-centered, faith-driven nonprofit in Bushbuckridge, South Africa. We empower youth and families through education, mentorship, and community programs rooted in faith and purpose.

What We Do

  • Motivation & Career Talks: Empowering learners with purpose and vision.
  • Church Programs: Youth worship nights, Sunday school, and family talks.
  • Community Campaigns: Addressing GBV, substance abuse, and mental health.
  • University Applications: Assisting with tertiary access, bursaries, and mentorship.

Mission, Vision & Values

Mission: Empower youth and families through holistic education, spiritual growth, and community support.

Vision: A purpose-driven generation transforming Bushbuckridge and beyond.

Core Values:

  • Faith – We walk with God at the center.
  • Compassion – We serve with love and empathy.
  • Empowerment – We believe in building capacity and confidence.
  • Integrity – We act with transparency and honesty.
  • Community – We grow stronger together.

Goals & Objectives

Short-Term Goals (2025–2026):

  • Partner with 20+ local schools.
  • Launch mentorship programs for youth and young women.
  • Run at least 4 annual social issue campaigns.

Long-Term Objectives:

  • Create a Youth Empowerment Centre.
  • Establish a sustainable bursary fund.
  • Grow a strong donor and volunteer network.
