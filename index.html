About Us

Miss G Foundation is a youth-centered, faith-driven nonprofit based in Bushbuckridge. We focus on education, mentorship, and community transformation through purpose and spiritual growth.

Programs

  • Motivation & Career Talks: Inspiring learners with subject and career guidance.
  • Church Programs: Youth worship, Sunday school, family development talks.
  • Community Campaigns: Awareness on GBV, substance abuse, and mental health.
  • University Help: Support for applications, bursaries, and learnerships.
