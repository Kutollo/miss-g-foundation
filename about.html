About SM's Foundation

Empowering youth & families with purpose, faith, and education.

Mission, Vision & Values

Mission

To inspire and equip youth and families to live purposefully and faithfully.

Vision

Building a generation of leaders grounded in love, wisdom, and faith.

Values

Faith, Integrity, Compassion, Empowerment, Excellence.

Goals & Objectives

  • Run youth empowerment programs monthly
  • Host family enrichment workshops quarterly
  • Promote spiritual growth through Bible-based teachings
  • Offer education and outreach to underserved communities

Opportunities Threats
